ABOUT VortiBD.COM

VortiBD.com is a convenient and useful platform where a candidate can apply for admission to VortiBD registered educational institute by creating a single account.

This sign up process is easy, anybody can open an account in a minute free of cost. Through VortiBD.com one can apply for admission every year during the admission period.

Any aspiring School / College / Madrasha / University / Education Institute can register here free of cost to maintain their online application and admission system. A dedicated team of VortiBD.com is always ready to extend cooperation for any institute interesred to digitalize and ease their end to end admission process.
